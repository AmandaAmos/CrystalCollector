Crystal Collector

A number will be randomly generated for you at the beginning of the game
The value of each crystal is hidden till the first time you click on it.
Click the crystals to add their amount to your total points
You win when your points match the random number.
You lose when your total points goes above the random number.
Each time the game is reset, the crystals are given new values.
